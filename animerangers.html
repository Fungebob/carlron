Codes for PerkSnaps: Rise to Power

Codes can either give Stat resets (or a stat refund “point”), Titles, Money $, or a certain duration of 2x Experience.

The duration of 2x Experience does stack and saves over rejoins.

helpHow to Redeem Codes

  1. Launch the game and open the main menu.
  2. Click on the "Codes" button (look for the gift icon).
  3. Enter the code in the text box and press "Redeem".
  4. Enjoy your rewards!
